<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>Grid Connected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar energy lamps can be categorized into two main market types: standalone and grid connected. Standalone solar lamps are designed to operate independently from the main power grid, making them ideal for remote areas or off-grid locations. They rely solely on solar power to generate electricity. On the other hand, grid connected solar lamps are connected to the main power grid and can supplement traditional electricity sources. They are suitable for urban areas where grid infrastructure is available and can help reduce overall energy costs.</p></p>
