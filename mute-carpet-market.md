<p><p>Mute carpet market is divided into different types based on their manufacturing process. Woven carpets are made by interlacing yarns in a crisscross pattern, providing durability and strength. Needle felt carpets are produced by electrostatic attraction of synthetic fibers, offering excellent sound absorption properties. Knotted carpets are handcrafted using traditional techniques, known for their intricate designs. Other types of mute carpets include tufted and flat weave carpets, each offering unique features and benefits for soundproofing and comfort in various spaces.</p></p>
