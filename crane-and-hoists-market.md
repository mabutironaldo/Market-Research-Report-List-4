<p><p>Manual hoists are operated by hand, providing a cost-effective and versatile solution for lifting and lowering light to medium loads. Electric hoists utilize an electric motor for efficient and precise operation, ideal for heavy-duty lifting tasks. Air hoists use compressed air as a power source, making them suitable for industrial applications where electricity is not available. Hydraulic hoists use hydraulic power to lift heavy loads with ease and precision, commonly found in construction and material handling industries. Each type offers unique advantages for different lifting needs.</p></p>
