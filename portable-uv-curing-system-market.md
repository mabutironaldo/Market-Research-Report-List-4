<p><ul><li>Spot-Type</li><li>Line-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable UV curing systems are categorized into two main types: Spot-Type and Line-Type. Spot-Type systems emit UV light in a concentrated area, making them suitable for curing small, precise spots. On the other hand, Line-Type systems emit UV light in a linear pattern, making them ideal for curing larger areas or continuous lines. Both types of systems are popular in industries such as electronics assembly, automotive, and medical device manufacturing for their efficiency and flexibility in curing UV adhesives, coatings, and inks.</p></p>
