<p><strong>The Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter</li><li>Passive Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>An Active Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter (EPD) continuously monitors and displays real-time radiation levels, providing instant feedback to the wearer. It is typically used in environments where radiation levels fluctuate frequently. A Passive EPD, on the other hand, records radiation dose over a certain period of time for later analysis. Passive EPDs are commonly used for compliance monitoring and dose recording purposes. Both types of EPDs play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of individuals working in radiation-prone environments.</p></p>
