<p><p>Thermal Silicone Grease is available in three main types: White, Gray, and Black. White Thermal Silicone Grease is typically non-conductive and is commonly used for electronic components to improve thermal conductivity. Gray Thermal Silicone Grease is electrically conductive and is used for components that require both thermal conductivity and electrical insulation. Black Thermal Silicone Grease is a combination of the two, offering both thermal conductivity and electrical insulation. Each type is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
