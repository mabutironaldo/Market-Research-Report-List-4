<p><p>A liftboat is a self-propelled, self-elevating vessel specifically designed for installating and maintaining offshore oil and gas platforms. The versatility and efficiency of liftboats make them a preferred choice for various offshore operations such as well intervention, platform maintenance, and wind turbine installation.</p><p>The global liftboat market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.4% during the forecast period.
