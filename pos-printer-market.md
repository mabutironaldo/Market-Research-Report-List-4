<p><ul><li>Impact type</li><li>Thermal type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>POS printers are categorized into two main types: Impact and Thermal. Impact printers work by striking a ribbon against the paper to produce text or images, making them suitable for environments where durability is important. On the other hand, Thermal printers use heat to produce text or images on special thermal paper, making them faster and quieter than impact printers. Impact printers are often preferred in industrial settings, while thermal printers are popular in retail and hospitality industries for their efficiency and ease of use.</p></p>
