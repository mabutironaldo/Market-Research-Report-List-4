<p><ul><li>Core Switch</li><li>Distribution Switches</li><li>Access Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data center switches are categorized into three main types: Core Switches, Distribution Switches, and Access Switches. Core switches are high-capacity switches that connect different parts of the data center network. Distribution switches aggregate connections from multiple access switches and connect them to the core switches. Access switches are responsible for connecting devices like servers and storage units to the rest of the network. Each type of switch plays a crucial role in ensuring efficient data transmission and connectivity within a data center environment.</p></p>
