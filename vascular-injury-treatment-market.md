<p><p>Vascular injury treatment market includes various types of procedures such as lateral arteriorrhaphy/venorrhaphy, patch angioplasty, bypass graft, extraanatomic bypass, thrombectomy, and others. Lateral arteriorrhaphy/venorrhaphy involves suturing the injured blood vessel. Patch angioplasty uses a patch to repair the damaged artery. Bypass graft involves using a graft to redirect blood flow around the injury. Extraanatomic bypass creates a new pathway for blood flow. Thrombectomy involves removing a blood clot from the vessel. Other treatments may include stenting or embolization.</p></p>
