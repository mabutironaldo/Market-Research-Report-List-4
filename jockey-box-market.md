<p><p>Jockey Box market offers two main types of cooling systems: Coil Cooler and Cold-plate Cooler. Coil Cooler uses a stainless steel coil submerged in ice water to chill beverages as they pass through. This system is efficient and provides quick cooling. On the other hand, Cold-plate Cooler uses a cold plate with chilled glycol running through it to cool beverages. This system is known for its uniform cooling properties and is often preferred for its reliability. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
