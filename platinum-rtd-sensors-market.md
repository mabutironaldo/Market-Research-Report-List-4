<p><strong>The Platinum RTD Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound RTD</li><li>Thin Film RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Platinum RTD Sensors market includes two main types: Wire-Wound RTD and Thin Film RTD sensors. Wire-Wound RTDs are made by winding a platinum wire around a core material, providing high accuracy and stability in temperature sensing. Thin Film RTDs, on the other hand, use a thin layer of platinum deposited on a substrate, offering fast response times and a smaller size compared to Wire-Wound RTDs. Both types are widely used in various industries for precise temperature measurements and monitoring.</p></p>
