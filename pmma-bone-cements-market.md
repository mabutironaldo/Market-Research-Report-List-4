<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity Cements</li><li>Medium Viscosity Cements</li><li>High Viscosity Cements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PMMA bone cements are commonly categorized into three types based on their viscosity levels - low, medium, and high. Low viscosity cements are fluid and easily injectable, making them ideal for minimally invasive procedures. Medium viscosity cements strike a balance between flowability and strength, suitable for a wide range of orthopedic applications. High viscosity cements are thicker and provide increased stability for complex or weight-bearing surgeries. Each type offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the patient and the procedure.</p></p>
