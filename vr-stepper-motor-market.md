<p><p>VR stepper motors are classified into two main types based on their power source: DC and AC. DC stepper motors are widely used in applications requiring precise control of position and speed, such as robotics and automation. They have a simple control mechanism and are highly efficient. On the other hand, AC stepper motors are commonly used in low-speed applications where high torque is required, such as in conveyor systems and industrial machinery. They are known for their reliability and durability in continuous operation.</p></p>
