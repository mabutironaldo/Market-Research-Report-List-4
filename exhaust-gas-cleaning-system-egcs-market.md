<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Cleaning System (EGCS) market includes three main types: Open Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System, Closed Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System, and Mixed Waste Gas Purification System. </p><p>Open Loop systems use seawater to scrub the exhaust gases, which is then discharged back into the ocean. Closed Loop systems use fresh water or a chemical solution to clean the exhaust gases, which is then treated and disposed of safely. Mixed Waste systems combine both open and closed loop technologies to provide efficient exhaust gas purification.</p></p>
