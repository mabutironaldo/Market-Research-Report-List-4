<p><p>A wall-mounted water purifier is a compact and efficient water filtration system that is fixed to the wall, providing clean and purified drinking water directly from the tap. These purifiers come with various filtration technologies such as RO (Reverse Osmosis), UV (Ultraviolet), and UF (Ultrafiltration) to remove impurities like bacteria, viruses, pesticides, and dissolved salts from the water.</p><p>The Wall-Mounted Water Purifier Market is witnessing significant growth due to increasing awareness about waterborne diseases and the need for access to safe drinking water. Rapid urbanization, industrialization, and deteriorating water quality are also driving the demand for water purifiers globally.
