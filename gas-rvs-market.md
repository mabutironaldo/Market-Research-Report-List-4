<p><p>Gas RVs are classified into two main market types - Motorized RVs and Towable RVs. Motorized RVs are built on a chassis that integrates the vehicle and living space, allowing for easy driving and setup. They are typically larger and provide more amenities, such as bathrooms and kitchens. Towable RVs, on the other hand, are trailers that are pulled behind a separate vehicle. They are generally more affordable and versatile, as they can be detached and left at a campsite while the towing vehicle is used for day trips.</p></p>
