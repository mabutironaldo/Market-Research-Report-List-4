<p><strong>The Front Load Parts Washers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water/Aqueous-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Front load parts washers are available in two main types: solvent-based and water/aqueous-based. Solvent-based parts washers use powerful solvents to remove grease, oil, and grime from parts. They are effective at cleaning heavily soiled parts but may pose health and environmental risks. On the other hand, water/aqueous-based parts washers use water-based detergents to clean parts without the use of harmful solvents. They are more environmentally friendly and safer for workers but may require longer cleaning times for particularly dirty parts.</p></p>
