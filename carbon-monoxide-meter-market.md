<p><p>Carbon Monoxide Meter Market offers three main types of sensors:</p><p>1. Biomimetic sensors mimic the way living organisms detect carbon monoxide. They use a gel that changes color when it detects the gas.</p><p>2. Electrochemical sensors use a chemical reaction to generate an electric current that is proportional to the concentration of carbon monoxide.</p><p>3. Metal Oxide Semiconductor sensors consist of a metal oxide film that detects carbon monoxide by changes in conductivity when the gas is present. Each type has its own strengths and limitations, catering to different market needs.</p></p>
