<p><ul><li>Straight Seam Welded Pipe</li><li>Helical Welded Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous welded pipe and tube market types include straight seam welded pipe and helical welded pipe. Straight seam welded pipe is produced by bending a steel plate and welding the edges together, resulting in a straight seam running along the length of the pipe. Helical welded pipe is produced by helically bending and welding a steel strip, creating a spiral seam along the pipe. Both types are widely used in various industries for transporting fluids and gases.</p></p>
