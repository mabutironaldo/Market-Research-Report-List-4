<p><p>The ATV coilover shock market can be categorized into two main types: Double Cylinder Type and Single Cylinder Type. Double Cylinder Type coilovers feature two separate cylinders for compression and rebound damping, providing increased adjustability and performance. On the other hand, Single Cylinder Type coilovers have a single cylinder for both compression and rebound damping, offering simplicity and ease of installation. Both market types cater to different consumer preferences for ATV suspension systems, offering a range of options for riders looking to upgrade their vehicle's performance.</p></p>
