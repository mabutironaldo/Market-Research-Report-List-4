Stimulation Devices use electrical or ultrasound stimulation to promote bone growth. BMPs are proteins that stimulate bone formation and repair. PRP involves injecting a concentration of platelets from the patient's own blood into the affected area to accelerate healing. These technologies offer non-invasive and effective solutions for promoting bone growth and treating fractures or bone defects.</p></p>
