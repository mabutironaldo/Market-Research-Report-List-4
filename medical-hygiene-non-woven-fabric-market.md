<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Medical Hygiene Non Woven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drylaid</li><li>Airlaid</li><li>Wetlaid</li><li>Spunlaid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical hygiene non-woven fabric is categorized into four main types: Drylaid, Airlaid, Wetlaid, and Spunlaid markets. Drylaid fabric is produced by laying fibers randomly and then bonding them together, while Airlaid fabric involves using air to distribute fibers onto a collecting surface. Wetlaid fabric is made by suspending fibers in a liquid and then forming the fabric, and Spunlaid fabric is created by spinning and bonding fibers together. Each type offers unique characteristics and is used in various medical hygiene applications.</p></p>
