<p><strong>The Home Ventilation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust Ventilation Systems</li><li>Supply Ventilation Systems</li><li>Balanced Ventilation Systems</li><li>Energy Recovery Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home ventilation systems aim to improve indoor air quality and overall comfort by removing stale air and introducing fresh air into living spaces. Exhaust ventilation systems remove stale air from the home, while supply ventilation systems bring in fresh outdoor air. Balanced ventilation systems combine both exhaust and supply systems for optimal air exchange. Energy recovery systems improve energy efficiency by transferring heat or coolness between incoming and outgoing air streams. Each type caters to different ventilation needs and preferences of homeowners.</p></p>
