<p><p>Industrial Gear Pump Market Types include Internal Gear Pumps and External Gear Pumps. Internal Gear Pumps have two gears involved in the pumping action, one inner and one outer, while External Gear Pumps have two meshing gears, one driving the other. Both types are commonly used in industrial applications due to their ability to efficiently pump liquids at high pressures and with a steady flow rate. They are known for their reliability, durability, and versatility in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical manufacturing, and food processing.</p></p>
