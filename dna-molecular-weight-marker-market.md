<p><ul><li>Below 50 base-pair (bp)</li><li>50 bp to 100 bp</li><li>100 bp to 1 kilo base-pair (kb)</li><li>1 kb to 5 kb</li><li>Above 5 kb</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DNA molecular weight markers are commonly categorized based on the size range they cover. Below 50 base-pair (bp) markers are used for small DNA fragments, while 50 bp to 100 bp markers cover a slightly larger range. The 100 bp to 1 kilo base-pair (kb) markers are ideal for most routine molecular biology applications. For larger DNA fragments, 1 kb to 5 kb markers are commonly used. Above 5 kb markers are specialized for larger DNA fragments, typically used in genomics and genome mapping studies.</p></p>
