<p><strong>The Car Gyroscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Axis</li><li>3 Axis</li><li>6 Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car gyroscope market is classified based on the number of axes they operate on - 2 Axis, 3 Axis, and 6 Axis. The 2 Axis gyroscope measures movement along two perpendicular axes, typically pitch and roll. The 3 Axis gyroscope can measure pitch, roll, and yaw movements. The 6 Axis gyroscope adds three more axes by including linear acceleration along each of the previous axes. Each type of gyroscope offers different levels of precision and functionality for applications in automotive stability control, navigation systems, and other motion sensing technologies.</p></p>
