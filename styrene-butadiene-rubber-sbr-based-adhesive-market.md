<p><p>Styrene-Butadiene Rubber (SBR) Based Adhesive market can be categorized into two main types: solution types and emulsion types. Solution types are adhesives in which SBR is dissolved in a solvent, providing a strong bond upon drying. Emulsion types, on the other hand, consist of SBR particles suspended in water, which form a bond once the water evaporates. Both types offer excellent adhesion properties and are widely used in industries such as construction, automotive, and packaging.</p></p>
