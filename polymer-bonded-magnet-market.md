<p><p>Polymer bonded magnets are available in two main types: injection magnet and pressed magnet markets. </p><p>Injection magnets are made using injection molding techniques, allowing for complex shapes and design flexibility. They are generally used in applications where customization and specific shapes are required. </p><p>Pressed magnets, on the other hand, are made using a compression molding process. They are ideal for high-volume production and are commonly used in more traditional applications where standard shapes and sizes are sufficient. Both types offer unique advantages based on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
