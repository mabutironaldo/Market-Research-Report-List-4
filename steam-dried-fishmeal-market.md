<p><p>Steam dried fishmeal is categorized into three main types based on the fat content present in the product. Defatted fish meal is the type with the lowest fat content, followed by semi-skimmed fish meal that contains a moderate amount of fat. Whole fish meal retains all the natural fats present in the fish during the drying process. Each type caters to different nutritional requirements for various applications in the aquaculture, animal feed, and fertilizer industries.</p></p>
