<p><p>Medical Grade Hydroxypropyl Cellulose (HPC) is available in two main types: L-HPC and H-HPC. L-HPC stands for Low-Substituted Hydroxypropyl Cellulose and is used primarily in pharmaceutical and food applications due to its high purity and low viscosity. On the other hand, H-HPC, or High-Substituted Hydroxypropyl Cellulose, is commonly used in the cosmetics industry for its film-forming and thickening properties. Both types of HPC are considered medical-grade and are valued for their biocompatibility and safety in various healthcare products.</p></p>
