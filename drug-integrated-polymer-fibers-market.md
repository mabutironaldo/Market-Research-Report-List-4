<p><p>Drug integrated polymer fibers market encompasses three main types of polymers - Polylactic Acid, Polydioxanone, and Polycaprolactone. Polylactic Acid is biodegradable and widely used in drug delivery systems. Polydioxanone offers high tensile strength and is often used in surgical sutures. Polycaprolactone is known for its biocompatibility and slow degradation rate, making it suitable for long-term drug release applications. Each type of polymer offers unique properties that cater to different requirements in drug delivery systems, leading to a diverse and evolving market.</p></p>
