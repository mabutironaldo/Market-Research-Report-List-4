<p><p>Pulse combustion dryers are commonly used in industrial settings to quickly and efficiently dry materials. There are three main types of pulse combustion dryers: spray dryers, rotary dryers, and pneumatic dryers. </p><p>Spray dryers use a spray nozzle to disperse the material into a hot gas stream, which quickly evaporates the moisture. Rotary dryers use a rotating drum to tumble and dry the material. Pneumatic dryers use a continuous flow of hot gas to convey and dry the material. Each type has its own benefits and uses in different industries.</p></p>
