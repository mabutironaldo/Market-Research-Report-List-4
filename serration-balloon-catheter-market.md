<p><p>Serration Balloon Catheter market is divided into two types based on the delivery method: Over-the-wire (OTW) Serration Balloon Catheter and Rapid Exchange Serration Balloon Catheter. OTW catheters have a longer balloon catheter shaft and are used for precise delivery in complex procedures. Rapid Exchange catheters have a shorter balloon catheter shaft and are easier to maneuver during balloon inflation. Both types offer unique advantages in different clinical scenarios, catering to the diverse needs of interventional cardiologists and radiologists.</p></p>
