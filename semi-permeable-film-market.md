<p><p>Semi-permeable film market consists of two main types - biomembrane and synthetic membrane. Biomembranes are composed of natural materials such as collagen, cellulose, or lipid bilayers, and are known for their biocompatibility and ability to mimic biological systems. Synthetic membranes, on the other hand, are man-made materials like polyethylene or polypropylene that offer high flexibility and durability. Both biomembrane and synthetic membrane markets serve various industries like healthcare, water treatment, and food packaging by providing selective permeability for different substances.</p></p>
