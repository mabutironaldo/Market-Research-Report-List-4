<p><p>The Motorcycle Inertial Measurement Unit Sensors market includes various types of sensors, with two prominent ones being Attitude and Heading Reference System (AHRS) and Inertial Navigation System (INS). AHRS sensors are used to determine the orientation of the motorcycle in three-dimensional space, while INS sensors track the position, orientation, and velocity of the motorcycle by using a combination of accelerometers and gyroscopes. These sensors play a crucial role in enhancing the stability, safety, and performance of motorcycles.</p></p>
