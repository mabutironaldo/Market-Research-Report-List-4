<p><ul><li>Incremental Magnetic Encoder</li><li>Absolute Magnetic Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic encoders are categorized into two main types: Incremental Magnetic Encoders and Absolute Magnetic Encoders. Incremental Magnetic Encoders generate a series of pulses as the encoder moves, providing relative position information. Absolute Magnetic Encoders, on the other hand, provide the exact position of the encoder at any given time, without needing a reference point. Both types are widely used in various industries for precise motion control and position sensing applications due to their accuracy, reliability, and durability.</p></p>
