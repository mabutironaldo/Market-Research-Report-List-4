<p><p>Slide modules are mechanical devices used for linear motion in various industrial applications. The market for slide modules can be classified into three main types based on the drive mechanism: Screw Drive Type, Belt Drive Type, and Other market. Screw drive type slide modules use threaded screw shafts to convert rotary motion into linear motion. Belt drive type slide modules utilize belts and pulleys for smooth and precise movement. The other market includes slide modules with different drive mechanisms such as rack and pinion, linear motors, or pneumatic drives.</p></p>
