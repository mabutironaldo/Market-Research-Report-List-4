<p><p>Additive Manufacturing Systems, commonly known as 3D printing, are categorized into two main markets: Polymer Additive Manufacturing Systems and Metal Additive Manufacturing Systems. Polymer Additive Manufacturing Systems utilize plastic materials to create 3D printed objects, making them more suitable for rapid prototyping and low-cost production. On the other hand, Metal Additive Manufacturing Systems use metals such as titanium, aluminum, and stainless steel to produce robust and high-quality parts for industries like aerospace, automotive, and healthcare. Both markets cater to different needs in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
