<p><p>Positron Emission Tomography (PET) System Market includes Direct Imaging, Indirect Imaging, and Alternative Imaging types. Direct Imaging involves the direct detection of emitted gamma rays from the patient, providing high-resolution images. Indirect Imaging utilizes a scintillator to convert gamma rays into visible light for detection. Alternative Imaging methods, such as Cerenkov luminescence imaging, offer complementary options for studying molecular processes in vivo.
