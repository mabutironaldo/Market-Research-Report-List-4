<p><p>Oil accumulators are classified into three types based on their internal construction: bladder type, piston type, and diaphragm type. Bladder type accumulators utilize a flexible bladder to separate oil and gas, providing smooth and efficient operation. Piston type accumulators use a piston to separate oil and gas, offering high pressure capabilities. Diaphragm type accumulators feature a flexible diaphragm to separate oil and gas, providing excellent gas and fluid separation. Each type of accumulator has unique characteristics and advantages for different applications.</p></p>
