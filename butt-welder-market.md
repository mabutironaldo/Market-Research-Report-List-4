<p><p>Butt welders are commonly found in three main types - mechanical, pneumatic, and hydraulic. Mechanical butt welders use a lever or cam mechanism to apply pressure and create the weld. Pneumatic butt welders use air pressure to generate the necessary force for welding. Hydraulic butt welders use hydraulic fluid to apply pressure and create the weld. Each type of butt welder offers unique advantages and is utilized in various industries based on the specific needs and requirements of the welding process.</p></p>
