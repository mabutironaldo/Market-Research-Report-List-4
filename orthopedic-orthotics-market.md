<p><p>Orthopedic orthotics market includes various types such as upper-limb orthoses, lower-limb orthoses, and spinal orthoses. Upper-limb orthoses are devices used to support and stabilize the upper extremities, while lower-limb orthoses provide support and alignment for the lower extremities. Spinal orthoses are designed to support and immobilize the spine, helping to treat spinal disorders or injuries. Each type of orthosis plays a crucial role in providing stability and mobility for individuals with musculoskeletal conditions or injuries.</p></p>
