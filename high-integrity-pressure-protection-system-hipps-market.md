<p><p>High-Integrity Pressure Protection System (HIPPS) market comprises of various types of components such as field initiators, logic solvers, valves, and actuators. Field initiators are responsible for detecting a pressure increase and initiating the shut-off process, while logic solvers process and analyze the data to determine the appropriate response. Valves then quickly shut off the flow of fluid to prevent overpressurization, and actuators physically actuate the valves. Each of these components plays a crucial role in ensuring the high integrity and safety of the system.</p></p>
