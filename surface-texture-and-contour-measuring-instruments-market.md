<p><p>Surface Texture and Contour Measuring Instruments market can be categorized into Contact and Non-Contact instruments. Contact instruments involve physical contact with the surface being measured, providing accurate results but may cause damage to delicate surfaces. On the other hand, Non-Contact instruments use non-destructive methods such as lasers or cameras to measure surface texture and contour, offering faster and safer measurements. Both types of instruments cater to different needs and preferences of industries and applications requiring accurate surface measurements.</p></p>
