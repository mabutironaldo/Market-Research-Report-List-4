<p><p>Vehicle mounted Digital Radiography is a growing market that includes two main types of systems: computed radiography (CR Tech) and direct radiography (DR Tech). CR Tech systems involve the use of a cassette-based imaging plate that is scanned to create digital images, while DR Tech systems use a digital detector to capture images directly. Both systems offer advantages in terms of image quality, speed, and efficiency for use in vehicles such as mobile X-ray units or portable imaging systems.</p></p>
