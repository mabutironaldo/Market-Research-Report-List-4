<p><ul><li>Stereolithography (SLA)</li><li>Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)</li><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Dental 3D Printer Market Types include Stereolithography (SLA), which uses a UV laser to cure liquid resin layer by layer, producing high-detail dental models. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) melts and extrudes thermoplastic material to build dental items. Digital Light Processing (DLP) uses a digital light projector to cure resin for precision dental prints. Other market types may include Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) and Continuous Liquid Interface Production (CLIP), each offering unique benefits for industrial dental printing.</p></p>
