<p><p>Electric lifting columns are commonly divided into two main types: multi-stage electric lifting columns and two-stage electric lifting columns. Multi-stage electric lifting columns have more adjustable height positions, allowing for a greater range of motion and flexibility in positioning. Two-stage electric lifting columns have a simpler design with two height settings, making them more suitable for basic up and down movements. Both types of electric lifting columns are commonly used in various applications such as standing desks, ergonomic workstations, and medical equipment.</p></p>
