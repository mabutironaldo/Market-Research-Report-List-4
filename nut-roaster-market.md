<p><p>In the nut roaster market, two main types dominate: Hot-Air Type and Direct-Fire Type. On the other hand, direct-fire type nut roasters utilize direct flames to roast nuts, providing a unique smoky flavor. Each type appeals to different consumer preferences, with hot-air type roasters being popular for their efficiency and consistency, while direct-fire type roasters are favored for the distinctive taste they impart to the nuts.</p></p>
