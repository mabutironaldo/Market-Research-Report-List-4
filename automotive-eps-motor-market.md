<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive EPS Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CEPS Motor: Conventional Electric Power Steering Motor is the traditional type of EPS motor that uses an electric motor connected to the steering column to assist with steering.</p><p>REPS Motor: Rack-assist Electric Power Steering Motor involves an electric motor directly mounted on the steering rack to provide steering assistance, resulting in a more responsive and agile steering feel.</p><p>PEPS Motor: Pinion-assist Electric Power Steering Motor utilizes an electric motor combined with a pinion gear to assist with steering, providing a balance between performance and efficiency.</p><p>EHPS Motor: Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering Motor combines electric and hydraulic systems to provide steering assistance, offering a mix of reliability and performance.</p></p>
