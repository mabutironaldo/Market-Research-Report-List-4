<p><p>Handheld refrigerant leak detectors are essential tools for detecting leaks in cooling systems. Infrared leak detectors use infrared technology to detect refrigerant gases by measuring the infrared radiation emitted by the gas molecules. Semiconductor leak detectors utilize semiconductor sensors to detect leaks by measuring changes in electrical conductivity in the presence of refrigerant gases. Both types offer portable and efficient solutions for detecting leaks in various applications such as HVAC systems, refrigeration units, and automotive air conditioning systems.</p></p>
