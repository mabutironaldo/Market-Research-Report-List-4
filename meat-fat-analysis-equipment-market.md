<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Meat fat analysis equipment market is segmented into two main types: DEXA technology and NIR technology. DEXA technology, or Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry, uses X-ray radiation to measure fat content in meat samples. On the other hand, NIR technology, or Near-Infrared Spectroscopy, uses infrared light to analyze fat composition. Both technologies are widely used in the food industry for accurate and efficient analysis of meat fat content, helping manufacturers ensure product quality and consistency.</p></p>
