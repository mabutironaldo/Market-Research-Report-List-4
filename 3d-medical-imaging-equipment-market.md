<p><p>3D medical imaging equipment includes X-ray, ultrasound, MRI, CT scan, and hybrid imaging technology. X-ray machines use electromagnetic radiation to produce images of the inside of the body. Ultrasound machines use sound waves to create images. MRI machines use magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images. CT scans combine X-ray and computer technology to create cross-sectional images. Hybrid imaging technology combines two or more imaging modalities to provide more comprehensive and accurate diagnostic information.</p></p>
