<p><p>Pine Honey is available in three main market types: Extracted Honey, Pressed Honey, and Comb Honey. Extracted Honey is obtained by spinning or straining the honey from the comb, resulting in a clear liquid. Pressed Honey involves crushing the honeycomb and draining the honey through a press, producing a thicker and richer texture. Comb Honey is sold in its original beeswax comb, providing a unique and untouched honey experience. These market types cater to different preferences and applications in the honey industry.</p></p>
