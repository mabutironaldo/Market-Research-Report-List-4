Alumina-based ceramics are strong and durable, suitable for long-span restorations. Lithium disilicate offers good aesthetics and strength, ideal for single crowns and small bridges. Zirconia is highly durable and biocompatible, often used for full-contour crowns and bridges. Each material type caters to different dental restoration needs, providing a range of options for dentists and patients.</p></p>
