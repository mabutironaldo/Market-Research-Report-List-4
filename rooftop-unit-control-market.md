<p><p>Rooftop Unit Control market is segmented into 2 Loop Unit and 4 Loop Unit types based on the number of control loops used for regulating the HVAC systems. 2 Loop Unit systems have two control loops for managing heating and cooling functions, while 4 Loop Unit systems have four loops for more precise control over temperature and humidity levels. Both types offer efficient control over rooftop units, but 4 Loop Unit systems are generally preferred for larger commercial buildings where more complex HVAC requirements are needed.</p></p>
