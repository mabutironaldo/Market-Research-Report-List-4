<p><p>ECG Equipment market includes various types of equipment such as Monitoring Equipment, Holter Monitoring Systems, Event Monitoring Systems, and Stress Testing Systems. Monitoring Equipment involves continuous monitoring of heart activity. Holter Monitoring Systems are portable devices that record heart activity over a 24-48 hour period. Event Monitoring Systems are used for longer-term monitoring of heart rhythm irregularities. Stress Testing Systems are used to assess heart function under physical stress. Each type of equipment serves a unique purpose in diagnosing and managing cardiac conditions.</p></p>
