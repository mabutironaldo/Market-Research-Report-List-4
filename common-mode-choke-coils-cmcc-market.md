<p><p>Common Mode Choke Coils (CMCC) are used to suppress electromagnetic interference in electronic circuits. The market for CMCCs is divided into two main types: SMD (Surface Mounted Device) Common Mode Choke Coils and Through Hole Common Mode Choke Coils. SMD CMCCs are smaller in size and are ideal for high-density applications, while Through Hole CMCCs are larger and offer better heat dissipation. Both types offer effective noise suppression capabilities and are widely used in a variety of electronic devices.</p></p>
