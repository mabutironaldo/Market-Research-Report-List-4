<p><p>Collagen artificial casings in the market are available in two main types: Edible collagen casings and Non-edible collagen casings. Edible collagen casings are designed to be consumed along with the products they encase, such as sausages. These casings are made of collagen derived from animal hides and are easily digestible. Non-edible collagen casings, on the other hand, are used primarily for products like pet food or cosmetics. These casings are not meant to be consumed and provide a barrier for the product inside while still being made of collagen material.</p></p>
