<p><p>Stainless Steel Semi-submersible Pumps come in two main types - Electric and Hydraulic. Electric pumps are powered by electricity and are suitable for applications where a power source is readily available. Hydraulic pumps, on the other hand, rely on hydraulic fluid to operate and are often used in environments where electricity is not easily accessible or where there are safety concerns. Both types of pumps are commonly used in various industries such as oil and gas, mining, and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
