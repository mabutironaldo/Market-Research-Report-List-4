<p><ul><li>Woven Single Coated Medical Tape</li><li>Non-woven Single Coated Medical Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single coated medical tape comes in two main types: woven and non-woven. Woven single coated medical tape is made from interlaced threads, creating a strong and durable tape that is suitable for securing heavy dressings or bandages. Non-woven single coated medical tape is made from synthetic fibers bonded together, providing a lightweight and breathable option for securing lighter dressings or for sensitive skin. Both types offer secure adhesion and gentle removal, making them essential products in the medical field.</p></p>
