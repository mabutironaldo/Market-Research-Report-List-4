<p><p>Document Management Market Types can be classified into On-premise and Cloud-based solutions. On-premise document management involves installing the software on company servers, allowing for complete control and security but requiring maintenance and IT support. Cloud-based document management, on the other hand, stores data on remote servers accessed through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. Both types cater to different business needs, with on-premise focusing on security and control, while cloud-based solutions prioritize accessibility and convenience.</p></p>
