<p><p>Cytotoxicity assay market includes various types of kits for assessing cell viability and toxicity levels. These include colorimetric, fluorometric, ELISA, crystal violet, and minimal inhibitory concentration assays. Colorimetric assays measure cell viability based on color changes, while fluorometric assays use fluorescent dyes. ELISA kits detect specific biomarkers of cytotoxicity, while crystal violet assays stain viable cells. Minimal inhibitory concentration assays determine the lowest concentration of a substance that inhibits cell growth. Each type of assay offers unique advantages for cytotoxicity testing.</p></p>
