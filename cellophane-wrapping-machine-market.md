<p><p>Cellophane wrapping machines come in three main market types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Semi-automatic machines require some supervision and assistance from an operator but are still efficient for medium-sized production runs. Manual machines are operated entirely by hand and are best suited for small-scale operations or for wrapping individual items. Each type of machine offers different levels of speed, efficiency, and automation to meet varying production needs.</p></p>
