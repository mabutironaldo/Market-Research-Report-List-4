<p><p>Geothermal heat pump systems can be categorized into three main market types based on their loop configurations: vertical closed loop, horizontal closed loop, and open loop. In vertical closed loop systems, pipes are installed deep underground in a vertical orientation. Horizontal closed loop systems involve horizontal trenches where pipes are buried. Open loop systems use water from a well or body of water for heat exchange. These different market types offer flexibility for various geographical and site-specific needs in utilizing geothermal energy for heating and cooling purposes.</p></p>
