<p><p>Whey protein ingredient market types include whey protein concentrate, whey protein isolate, and hydrolyzed whey protein. Whey protein concentrate is a less processed form containing some fat and lactose, while whey protein isolate is more processed with higher protein content and minimal fat and lactose. Hydrolyzed whey protein is further processed to break down protein chains for easier digestion. Each type offers varying levels of protein purity and processing, catering to different consumer preferences and dietary needs.</p></p>
