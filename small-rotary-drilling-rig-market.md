<p><p>Small Rotary Drilling Rig Market encompasses two major types: Crawler and Rubber-Tired. The choice between these two types depends on the specific needs of the drilling project, with Crawler rigs excelling in rugged terrains and Rubber-Tired rigs being more suitable for urban areas.</p></p>
