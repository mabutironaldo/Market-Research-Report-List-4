<p><p>Soil aerators are tools used to improve soil structure by breaking up compacted soil and allowing for better air and water circulation. There are two main types of soil aerators: mechanical and pneumatic. Mechanical soil aerators use rotating blades or tines to penetrate the soil, while pneumatic aerators use compressed air to create small holes in the ground. Both types are effective in promoting healthy root growth and improving overall soil health in gardens, lawns, and agricultural land.</p></p>
