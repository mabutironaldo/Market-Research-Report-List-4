<p><ul><li>Invasive Devices</li><li>Minimally-invasive Devices</li><li>Non-invasive Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cardiac output monitoring market includes three types of devices: invasive, minimally-invasive, and non-invasive. Invasive devices are used internally with catheters inserted into blood vessels. Minimally-invasive devices involve external sensors placed on the body to measure cardiac output. Non-invasive devices use technologies like echocardiography or bioimpedance to assess cardiac function without invading the body. Each type of monitoring device offers varying levels of invasiveness and accuracy, catering to different patient needs and healthcare settings.</p></p>
