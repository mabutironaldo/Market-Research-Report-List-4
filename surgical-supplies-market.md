<p><p>The surgical supplies market can be divided into different types such as cutting instruments, grasping instruments, retractors, needle holders, surgical gloves, and surgical lines. Cutting instruments are used for making incisions, while grasping instruments help surgeons hold and manipulate tissues. Retractors are used to hold back tissues and organs during surgery. Needle holders are used to hold and manipulate needles during suturing. Surgical gloves are worn to maintain sterility. Surgical lines are used for suturing and tying off blood vessels or tissues during surgery.</p></p>
