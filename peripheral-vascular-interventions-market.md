<p><p>Peripheral Vascular Interventions involve various medical devices such as stents, balloons, and guide wires that are used to treat blockages or narrowing of blood vessels outside of the heart. Stents are mesh-like tubes that are placed in the blood vessel to support its walls and keep it open, while balloons are used to inflate and widen the vessel. Guide wires are thin, flexible wires used to navigate through the blood vessels and deliver the stent or balloon to the desired location.</p></p>
