<p><p>IV connector without needle market types include Positive Fluid Displacement, Negative Fluid Displacement, and Neutral Displacement. Positive Fluid Displacement connectors push fluid into the IV tubing, ensuring a secure connection. Negative Fluid Displacement connectors prevent air from entering the IV tubing during connection. Neutral Displacement connectors allow for easy fluid transfer without any additional pressure. Each type offers unique benefits for healthcare providers and patients, catering to different needs and preferences in the medical field.</p></p>
