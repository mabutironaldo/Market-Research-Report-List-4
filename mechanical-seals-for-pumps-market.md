<p><p>Mechanical seals for pumps are available in three main types: single end, double-sided, and multi-faceted. Single end seals are the most basic type, featuring one seal face and one mating ring. Double-sided seals have two seal faces and provide increased reliability and efficiency. Multi-faceted seals have multiple seal faces and are designed for higher pressure and temperature applications. Each type of seal caters to different pump performance requirements and industries, offering a versatile range of options for various applications.</p></p>
