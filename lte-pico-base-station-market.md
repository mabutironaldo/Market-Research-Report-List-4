<p><ul><li>Time Division Duplex (TDD)</li><li>Frequency Division Duplex (FDD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LTE Pico Base Station market types are divided into Time Division Duplex (TDD) and Frequency Division Duplex (FDD) markets. TDD technology uses a single frequency band for both uplink and downlink communication, with time slots alternating between the two. On the other hand, FDD technology uses separate frequency bands for uplink and downlink communication. TDD is more efficient in utilizing spectrum resources, while FDD offers better interference management and coverage. Both markets cater to different needs and requirements in the LTE Pico Base Station industry.</p></p>
