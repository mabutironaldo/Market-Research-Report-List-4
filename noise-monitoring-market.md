Short-Term Monitoring involves collecting noise data for a limited period, such as during an event or construction project. Long-Term Monitoring involves monitoring noise levels over an extended period, typically for regulatory compliance or environmental impact assessments. Permanent Monitoring involves continuous monitoring of noise levels in a specific area, usually for ongoing environmental health and safety purposes.</p></p>
