<p><p>Powered Surgical Instruments Market consists of three main types based on their power source: Electric-powered, Battery-powered, and Pneumatic-powered. Electric-powered instruments are connected to a power source directly for continuous operation. Battery-powered instruments offer greater flexibility and mobility as they are not restricted by cords. Pneumatic-powered instruments utilize compressed air to function, making them suitable for use in environments where electricity is not readily available. Each type has its own advantages and is preferred based on the specific needs and requirements of the surgical procedure.</p></p>
