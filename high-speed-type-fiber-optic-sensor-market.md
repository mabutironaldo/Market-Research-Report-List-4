These sensors are ideal for applications that require high precision and low attenuation. On the other hand, multimode fiber optic sensors use multiple transmission modes, allowing them to carry multiple signals simultaneously. They are commonly used in short-distance applications where high data transfer rates are not critical. Both types of fiber optic sensors play a crucial role in the high-speed data transmission market.</p></p>
