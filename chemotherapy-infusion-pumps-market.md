<p><p>Chemotherapy infusion pumps are devices used to deliver medication directly into a patient's bloodstream during cancer treatment. There are three main types of infusion pumps used in the market: Large Volume Pump (LVP) which delivers medication in large volumes, Syringe Pump which delivers medication in small, precise doses, and Elastomeric Pumps which use an elastic membrane to push medication into the patient's bloodstream. These pumps are crucial in ensuring precise and controlled delivery of chemotherapy drugs to maximize treatment effectiveness and minimize side effects.</p></p>
