<p><ul><li>Air Cooling</li><li>Direct Cold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Supermarket freezers can be classified into two main market types: air cooling and direct cold. Air cooling freezers use a forced-air circulation system to evenly distribute cold air throughout the unit, ensuring consistent temperatures and optimal food preservation. On the other hand, direct cold freezers rely on a direct cooling system where cold air is blown directly onto the food items. Both market types have their own benefits and drawbacks, such as energy efficiency and temperature control.</p></p>
