<p><p>The Stainless Steel Surgical Scalpel market is divided into two main types: Reusable Surgical Scalpel and Disposable Surgical Scalpel. On the other hand, Disposable Surgical Scalpels are meant for single-use only, eliminating the risk of cross-contamination and the need for sterilization. Disposable Scalpels are preferred in situations where sterility is critical or when convenience is prioritized over cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
