<p><p>Vertical CNC machining centers are classified based on the number of axes they operate on. The most common types include 3-axis, 4-axis, 5-axis, and 7-axis machines. The 3-axis machines move in three directions, while 4-axis machines allow for rotation around a vertical axis. 5-axis machines add the ability to tilt and rotate the cutting tool, providing greater flexibility. 7-axis machines further improve precision by allowing for additional movement in multiple directions. Other types of vertical CNC machining centers exist as well, offering unique capabilities tailored to specific applications.</p></p>
