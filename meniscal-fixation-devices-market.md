<p><p>Meniscal fixation devices are used in orthopedic surgery to repair meniscal tears. There are several types of these devices available in the market including arrows, screws, darts, and others. Arrows are designed to secure the meniscus in place with barbs or flanges. Screws provide compression and fixation to the meniscus. Darts are inserted into the meniscus to hold it in place. Other types of fixation devices may include staples, tacks, or sutures. Each type of device offers unique advantages in terms of ease of use, stability, and patient outcomes.</p></p>
