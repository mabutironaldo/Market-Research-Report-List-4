<p><p>Packaged Basmati Rice market can be divided into two main types: Dry Basmati Rice and Parboiled Basmati Rice. </p><p>Dry Basmati Rice is processed by removing the outer husk and retaining the bran layer, resulting in long-grain rice with a fluffy texture when cooked. On the other hand, Parboiled Basmati Rice is first partially boiled in the husk before milling, which helps retain more nutrients in the grains and gives it a firmer texture when cooked. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and cooking needs in the packaged rice market.</p></p>
