<p><ul><li>Plug-in Relay</li><li>PCB Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micro Automotive Relay market can be classified into two main types: plug-in relays and PCB relays. Plug-in relays are designed to easily insert into a socket or connector, making them convenient for installation and replacement. PCB relays, on the other hand, are mounted directly onto printed circuit boards, saving space and reducing wiring complexity. Both types of relays offer compact size and high reliability, making them essential components in automotive applications that require efficient electrical switching.</p></p>
