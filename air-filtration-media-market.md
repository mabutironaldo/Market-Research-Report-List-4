<p><ul><li>Spunbond</li><li>Needlefelt</li><li>Wet laid</li><li>Melt blown</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air filtration media market consists of various types including spunbond, needlefelt, wet laid, and melt blown. Spunbond media is made of continuous filaments bonded together to provide high strength and efficiency. Needlefelt media is created by mechanically punching fibers together to form a dense filter. Wet laid media involves a water-based process to bond fibers together. Melt blown media is formed by blowing molten polymer through high-velocity air, creating fine fibers for effective filtration. Each type offers unique characteristics and applications in air filtration systems.</p></p>
