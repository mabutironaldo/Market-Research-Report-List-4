<p><p>Centrifugal Chemical Process Pump Market can be divided into two main types based on their design and application - End Suction and Back Pull. End Suction pumps are used for general industrial applications where the liquid is pumped in through the end of the casing. On the other hand, Back Pull pumps are designed specifically for processes that involve high temperatures, corrosive fluids, or viscous materials, and have a unique design that allows for easy maintenance and repair without disassembling the pump from the piping system.</p></p>
