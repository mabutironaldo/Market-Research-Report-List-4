<p><strong>The Lift Dumper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Backward Tipping</li><li>Lateral Tipping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lift dumper market offers two main types: backward tipping and lateral tipping. Backward tipping dumpers have a tipping mechanism that raises the front of the machine to pour materials out from the back, making them suitable for confined spaces and tight working conditions. On the other hand, lateral tipping dumpers have a mechanism that allows the dump to move sideways, making them ideal for dumping materials alongside trenches or other obstacles. Both types offer versatility and efficiency in various construction and industrial applications.</p></p>
