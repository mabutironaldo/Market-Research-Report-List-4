<p><p>Rotary stepper motors can be classified into three main types: Permanent Magnet (PM), Variable Reluctance (VR), and Hybrid (HB). PM motors use permanent magnets to generate a magnetic field, VR motors rely on the principle of varying reluctance to produce motion, and HB motors combine features of both PM and VR motors for improved performance. Each type has its own unique characteristics and advantages, making them suitable for different applications in the market.</p></p>
