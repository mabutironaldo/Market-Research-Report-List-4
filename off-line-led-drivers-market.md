<p><p>Off-line LED drivers can be categorized into two main types based on their input power source: DC power and AC power drivers. DC power drivers are suitable for applications where a DC power source is readily available, such as in automotive or battery-powered devices. On the other hand, AC power drivers are designed to work with standard AC power sources commonly found in households and commercial buildings. Both types of drivers play a crucial role in providing efficient and reliable power management for LED lighting systems.</p></p>
