<p><p>Low-frequency speakers are categorized into different types based on their design and functionality. Woofer speakers are designed to reproduce low-frequency sounds with high power handling capabilities. Midwoofer speakers are a hybrid between mid-range and woofer speakers, offering a balance between low and mid-range frequencies. Subwoofer speakers are dedicated solely to reproducing deep bass frequencies, enhancing the overall audio experience. Rotary subwoofers use a unique rotating design to produce powerful and deep bass frequencies, allowing for a more immersive sound experience. Each type of low-frequency speaker caters to different audio needs and preferences.</p></p>
