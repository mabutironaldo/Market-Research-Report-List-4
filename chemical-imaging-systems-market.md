<p><p>Chemical imaging systems are used to analyze the chemical composition and structure of materials. Different types of chemical imaging systems include spectrochemical imaging microscope, confocal Raman microscope, atomic force microscope, dispersive microscope, and others. Spectrochemical imaging microscope uses spectroscopic techniques to image chemical composition. Confocal Raman microscope combines confocal microscopy with Raman spectroscopy to provide high-resolution chemical imaging. Atomic force microscope uses a sharp probe to image surface properties at the nanoscale. Dispersive microscope uses dispersion techniques to analyze chemical composition. Other types include different techniques for chemical imaging.</p></p>
