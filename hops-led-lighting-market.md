Vertical Light, on the other hand, involves placing light fixtures vertically along the sides of the growing area to promote even growth and maximize space efficiency. Both types of lighting solutions are designed to improve yields, increase energy efficiency, and optimize growing conditions for hops cultivation.</p></p>
